# Remote Pairing Guide

Follow these steps to pair your Tncglf motor with the remote control.

### Step 1: Power On
Ensure the motor is connected to a power source (Battery or DC adapter).

### Step 2: Programming Mode
1. Locate the **P1 button** on the motor head.
2. Press and hold the P1 button for about **2 seconds** until the motor jogs (a brief up and down movement).

### Step 3: Pair the Remote
1. Within 10 seconds, press and hold the **STOP** button on the remote.
2. The motor will jog again, confirming the pairing is successful.

> **Note:** If the motor does not jog, please check the battery level of your remote.
